2. How to Diagnose Plant Diseases

Step 1: Take Photo

Take a clear photo of the affected plant. Include healthy parts for comparison.

Step 2: Upload Image

Click "Upload Image" and select your photo. JPEG, PNG, or GIF formats supported.

Step 3: Get Results

Our AI analyzes the image and provides disease identification .

Tips for Better Results:
  • Ensure good lighting - avoid shadows and glare
  • Focus on the affected areas (spots, lesions, discoloration)
  • Include healthy parts of the plant for comparison
  • Take multiple photos from different angles
  • Maximum file size: 10MB
